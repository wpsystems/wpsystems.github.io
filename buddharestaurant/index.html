About us

B uddha’s Vegan Restaurant is one of Toronto’s earliest Asian Vegan Restaurant. We adhere to a goal of serving tasty, healthy and plant-based cuisines. We believe that eating healthy is not only good for our body but also for our mind since a peaceful mind comes from a tranquil body; thus, Buddha's Vegan Restaurant is trying to connect people to a compassionate and pleasant lifestyle.
